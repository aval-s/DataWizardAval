## Featured Projects

### 🍔 Fine-Tuning Showdown: Food-101 Vision Models
Collaborative deep learning study comparing fine-tuning strategies across **ResNet-18, EfficientNetV2-S, and ViT** on the Food-101 dataset (101 classes, 100K images).
- ViT with LayerNorm tuning hit **85.6% accuracy** training only 58K parameters
- Tested robustness on blur, noise, occlusion, and downsampled inputs
- Grad-CAM heatmaps reveal how each strategy attends spatially
- LoRA on the head alone struggled across all three architectures

→ [View on GitHub](https://github.com/aval-s/fine-tuning_Food101)

### 💧 Underground Water Level Prediction
Time series forecasting model predicting underground water levels using ARIMA & SARIMA with a 5-year forecast horizon and a Tkinter UI.

→ [View on GitHub](https://github.com/aval-s/Underground-Water-Level-Prediction)
